* My feet 
* follow 
* the sound 
* of
* a heartbeat 
* skipping
* along 
* a foreign 
* road 
* leading me 
* toward 
* a horizon
* where stillness finds
* itself
* in the rhythm 
* of quiet 
* footsteps
* on amber 
* landscapes
* praising the sun.
