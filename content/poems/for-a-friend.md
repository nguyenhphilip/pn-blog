* *Meeting Again: For Alesha*
* {{< poem-space >}}
* You asked if I ever
* wrote a poem about you
* And I said never
* Not without another few
* Days of this again:
* {{< poem-space >}}
* Us by the lake
* Unsure
* of what to make
* of all this time
* between us.
