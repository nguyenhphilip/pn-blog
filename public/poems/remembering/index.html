Remembering

  • A Black birder,
  • subjected to
  • attempted murder
  • by words
  • so calculated
  • that even
  • the birds
  • could no longer
  • sing–

  • A Black woman,
  • shot dead
  • in her bed
  • holding dreams
  • that will
  • never
  • find their way
  • to this harsh
  • reality–

  • A Black man,
  • his body pinned
  • under
  • the flagpole
  • of a white
  • Knee–
  • A body
  • ripped
  • of breath–
  • A life stripped
  • of color–
  • Killed–
  • by and with
  • a weapon
  • deadlier than History–
