Singularity

    • Singularity

    • We were in this together
    • Once–Inseparable
    • like time and gravity,

    • Our love

    • A singularity
    • That even the Big Bang
    • Grew jealous of.

    • Was it inevitable then
    • That we would drift

    • Apart? You

    • At the speed of light,

    • I

    • The cold crawl
    • Of Pluto.

    • My hope rests
    • In cosmic equations,
    • Promises

    • Written on the sky.

    • It’s out of the question:
    • I know we’ll be together
    • Once the show is over

    • And begins again.

    • But until then
    • There is only

    • This

    • And less
    • On the trail of your blazing star.
