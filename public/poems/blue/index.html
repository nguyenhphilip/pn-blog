Blue

    • Blue

    • I walked to the lake
    •   to skip
    •    stones
    •     the way people do

    • with their thoughts
    •   when they at last notice
    •    that their pockets
    •     are full.

    • Then you honked at me
    •   in your hurried voice
    •    Good morning - Good morning!
    •     and invited me to linger

    • as your webbed feet waltzed
    •   toe by toe
    •    across the sand
    •     into the wild open blue
