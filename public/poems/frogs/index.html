Frogs

  • Find me
  • on the forest floor
  • lingering
  • with the song
  • of frogs
  • leaping
  • from one stone
  • to the next
  • preaching words
  • of wonder
  • under shadows
  • and
  • sunlit beams
  • of self-forgetting.
