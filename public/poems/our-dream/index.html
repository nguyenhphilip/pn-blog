Our Dream

  • The rainbow is our dream
  • our sky sketched from creation,
  • our soul canvas colored by
  • dancing constellations.
  • A place open to all
  • (if you’re not
  • afraid of heights)
  • A home for two who
  • craft dreams
  • lit by candlelight.
